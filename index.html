harvey

Harvey is an agent similar to Claude Code but written in Go and designed to use Ollama server to access large models locally or if configurated access publicai.co. It is a terminal based application.

The Harvey name is inspired by the Mary Chase play of the same name. My little agent runs on small computers but is available to those who choose to see its value. Harvey in the play was a Púca, a mythic Celtic spirit who at times was proned to mischief. One those who the Púca chose to let seem it would see and even then they had to be willing to see it.

I think the current AI hype cycle will likely end with a bang and a crash but the open models will still be there to use. I think they just need to run on smaller computers that are more engery friendly and under an individual person’s control. A computer program, a language model, isn’t human and we should not expect them to be responsible. That’s something that requires us humans to do.
